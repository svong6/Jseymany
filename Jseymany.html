About Jenny

Jenny Seymany loves establishing and maintaining long-lasting relationships with her real estate clients. From first-time homebuyers to seasoned homeowners, Jenny nurtures genuine relationships as she partners with you for success in your next home purchase or sale.

Jenny’s strong customer service background gives her an advantage when caring for your real estate needs. She quickly aligns with your goals and delivers strategies that achieve success.

Jenny infuses her solutions-driven mindset into each interaction as she maintains an environment of calm and ease for her clients through the highs and lows of every real estate transaction she facilitates.

Licensed since 2012, Jenny understands the importance of fostering trust with her clients throughout the entire real estate experience — and beyond. Not only does Jenny ensure you have a memorable and exciting journey of buying or selling your home, she ensures you have a trusted partner for life for all your real estate needs, now and in the future.

Jenny currently resides in Murrieta, California. In her free time, she enjoys traveling and taking road trips as often as she can. She loves exploring new places, visiting art museums, trying new foods, and meeting new people. More than that, she loves coming home to her two dogs, Opie and Jax.
